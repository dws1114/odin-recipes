Mashed Potatoes

This recipe for mashed potatoes is heavenly! It also is not for the feint of heart, because it uses lots of butter and heavy cream to make it sing.

The secret to smooth potatoes is using a potato ricer. Trust me, you will want to invest in this essential tool to impress all of your friends.

Ingredients

  • 4 large russet potatoes
  • 2 tbls butter
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • salt

Steps

  1. Peel, cut and rinse potatoes. It is important to keep cuts fairly even in size.
  2. Put potates in large pot and fill with cold water 1 inch above potatoes.
  3. Bring water to a boil.
  4. Salt the water and stir.
  5. Bring to a simmer and continue to cook until potatoes are tender, 30-40 minutes.
  6. Drain potatoes.
  7. Rice the potatoes using a potatoes ricer into large pot.
  8. Heat butter and heavy cream in small saucepot. Do not boil.
  9. Mix cream and butter into riced potatoes.
  10. Season with salt as needed.
