Spaghetti With Meat Sauce

This is essential classic spaghetti with homemade meat sauce. I eat this at least twice a month. I can't get enough of it!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb dried spaghetti. I recommend DeCecco.
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 15oz can tomato sauce
  • 1 15oz can diced tomatoes
  • 4 cloves garlic minced
  • 1 half onion diced
  • 2 tbls olive oil
  • Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. On medium/high heat saute onions in olive oil until soft.
  2. Add garlic to pan and saute briefly until fragrant, do not burn.
  3. Add diced tomatoes to pan and bring to simmer.
  4. Stir often and heat until water is mostly evaporated.
  5. Add tomato sauce.
  6. In seperate pan, on medium heat, cook ground beef fully.
  7. Drain ground beef and combine it into the tomato sauce. Turn heat to low to remain warm.
  8. In large pot, boil salted water.
  9. Cook dry pasta according to package instructions.
  10. Top cooked spaghetti with meat sauce (Parmesan cheese recommended).
